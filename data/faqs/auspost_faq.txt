# Delivery FAQs (condensed for prototype)

Q: Can I opt out of Safe Drop?
A: Yes, change preferences in MyPost or the AusPost app.

Q: Will alcohol be left at the door?
A: No. Items requiring ID cannot be left with ATL; they must be collected in person.

Q: What happens if no one is home and there is no safe place?
A: The parcel will be taken to a nearby Post Office or Parcel Locker for collection.

Q: Can preferences differ by parcel?
A: Yes, per-parcel preferences can be set in the app when available.

Q: What evidence will I get for Safe Drop?
A: Photo proof or a delivery record is provided where available.
